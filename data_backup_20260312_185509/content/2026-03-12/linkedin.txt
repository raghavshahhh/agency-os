🌟 Ever faced a project where the solution wasn't obvious at the start? That's what happened during our work with ACME Corp. 🌟

We were tasked with creating a custom AI chatbot to streamline customer inquiries. Initially, we thought a straightforward approach would do the trick. After all, it's just about natural language processing, right?

But we hit a roadblock when the chatbot couldn't understand the nuances of customer queries. Here’s what we learned:

- **Start with user personas**: Understanding the typical customer interactions is crucial. We didn’t fully grasp the varied ways customers would phrase their questions.
- **Iterative testing**: We needed to test the chatbot with real users and gather feedback quickly. Our initial approach was too theoretical, and we missed key pain points.

Personal insight: This experience taught me that no matter how tech-savvy we are, user empathy is key. It’s not just about the code; it’s about the problem it solves.
